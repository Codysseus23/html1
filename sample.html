How I got to where I am today.

I am originally from Sweet Home Alabama. Specifically the Madison/Huntsville area near the Space and Rocket Center.
My dad got me interested in tech very early in my life but I didn't consider a career in it for a while.
After High School, I went to the University of Alabama, despite being an Auburn fan, because they offered a full ride in the field I was interested in, Management Information Systems.
I ended up not completing college and moved back home for a couple of years until I met my now fiance and moved to GA for her.
I then actually found out about this class from a friend I play Magic with and he suggested I look into the boot camp and I am glad I did for many reasons.

  • It got me out of a rut I had been stuck in.
  • It gave me confidence to work towards a better me and a career I am interested in.
  • The bootcamp style seemed right up my alley for how I learn.

I am excited for the journey ahead becasue this cohort has been nothing short of wondrous!
I can't tell you how much y'all have helped me. Once again, thank you so much to the teachers and fellow students.
